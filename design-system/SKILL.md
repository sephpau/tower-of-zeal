## Quick reference

- **Colors:** MoTZ Red `#FF2A55`, Winner Gold `#FBBF24`, Electric Purple `#7000FF`, Void Black `#0F0518`. Tokens in `colors_and_type.css`.
- **Type:** Fredoka (display, substitute for Yourmate) + Inter (body, substitute for Roc Grotesk) + JetBrains Mono (HUD/labels).
- **Mascot:** Ego — narrator and centerpiece. Subject must never be overpowered by background. Art in `assets/mascot/`.
- **Default vibe:** dark "void mode" with red/purple radial glow backdrops, grid overlay, glass surfaces, gradient text, gold for wins.
- **Tone:** confident, playful, gamer-first. *Fam, Squad, Legends.* Web3 vocab (Mint, Vault, Drop) used naturally. Exclamation points earned, not stacked.
- **UI kit:** `ui_kits/website/` — recreation of the markofthezeal.com hub. Copy components from there.
